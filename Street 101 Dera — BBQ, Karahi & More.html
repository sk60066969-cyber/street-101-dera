Our Philosophy

Three ingredients,
used well.

Street 101 Dera started as a single charcoal grill on a busy street corner and a conviction that most dishes have too much going on. We buy whatever the butchers bring in that morning, grill it hard, and get out of its way.

Every plate that leaves the kitchen has touched the fire — grilled, roasted, smoked, or licked with flame at the very end. It's a slower way to cook. We think it's the only way that tastes honest.

  • Live-fire only — charcoal grill, tandoor, zero shortcuts
  • Fresh daily — meat and spices sourced every morning
  • Family-style plates — built for a table that shares
